What is Robots Decide Litmag

This is a literary magazine where robots select the poems that are submitted. Anyone can submit a poem, but only the ones chosen by the robots will be published. Robots Decide Litmag is the first automated litmag. It is a lighthearted experiment to show the shortcomings of artificial intelligence, and just to have some fun. It instantly gives you an acceptance/denial and adds your piece to the next issue. Please understand that this is not meant to endorse AI replacing people. That is bad.
